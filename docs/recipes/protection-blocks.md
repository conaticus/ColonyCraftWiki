# Protection Blocks

Protection blocks can be used to protect chests inside your claims from being raided by other players.

### Weak Protection Block

- 4x Limonite Ore
- 1x Redstone

### Medium Protection Block

- 4x Acanthite Ore

### Strong Protection Block

- 4x Valtronium Ore
- 1x Amethyst Cluster
